Modesta

#CLOTHES FOR THE COMING TREND

Modesta is a boutique that curates unique and exclusive pieces from socially
fashion designers and ethical women’s cooperatives based all across Africa, responsible
fashion brands inspired by Africa. We offer fashion conscious consumers beautifully
hand-crafted pieces from talented fashion designers and ethical women’s cooperatives based all across Africa


At the heart of our business is the platform we have created for African-inspired designers
to showcase and market their creations to the world Through Modesta, we are promoting a positive economic impact within their communities while
redefining “African” fashion abroad.
